Photography — 2025

MODEL SHOOT

Fashion photography

5

Concept

A fashion photography project that strips away the excess of commercial image-making to find something more essential: the relationship between the body, the garment, and the light that falls between them.

Shot on high-contrast black and white film with a single studio light source, the images prioritise form over decoration. The model's presence is amplified rather than objectified — each frame is negotiated, collaborative, deliberate.

Process

Pre-production involved detailed mood boarding, garment selection, and multiple test shoots to dial in the lighting setup. The shoot itself was directed and styled personally. Post-production was minimal — grain retained, contrast pushed, no heavy retouching.
