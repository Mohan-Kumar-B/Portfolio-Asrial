About the Designer

Crafting narratives
through cloth
& concept.

I am a fashion design student driven by the belief that clothing is a visual language — one that tells stories, challenges conventions, and holds a mirror to society. My work lives at the intersection of art, culture, and craft.

Each collection I build is rooted in research: historical archives, social discourse, literary symbolism. Whether working in a studio draping environment or conceptualising an editorial shoot, I pursue authenticity above all else.
